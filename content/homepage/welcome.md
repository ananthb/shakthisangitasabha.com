Shakthi Sangita Sabha is dedicated to fostering and preserving the rich traditions of Carnatic music and classical Indian dance. Our acoustically-designed auditorium provides the perfect setting for kutcheris, arangetrams, and cultural celebrations that honor these timeless art forms.
